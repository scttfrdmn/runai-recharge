Idle and unbilled are not the same thing and must never be summed. Idle is capacity nobody asked for — an operational fact you manage with scheduling and scale-to-zero, and on pre-purchased capacity it is money that was already spent. Unbilled is allocated GPU-hours that fell out of every statement because of a missing pool_class mapping or a missing rate. That is a bug, it is fixable with an INSERT, and until it is fixed somebody used a GPU and nobody paid for it.

Capacity is sampled at the poll interval, so a node that lived and died between two polls is invisible here. Allocation is exact.
